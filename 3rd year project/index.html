My FAQ Resolution

What services are provided by Anganwadi Sevikas??

Anganwadi Sevikas offer a range of services including health check-ups for pregnant women and children, immunizations, nutritional support through supplementary feeding programs, preschool education for children aged 3 to 6 years, and awareness programs on health, hygiene, and childcare practices for parents and caregivers.

How do Anganwadi Sevikas promote health and nutrition??

Anganwadi Sevikas promote health and nutrition through various activities such as conducting regular health check-ups, monitoring growth and development of children, distributing nutritious meals and supplements, organizing awareness sessions on proper nutrition and hygiene practices, and providing counseling and support to pregnant women and mothers on breastfeeding, weaning, and dietary diversity.

What training and support do Anganwadi Sevikas receive??

Anganwadi Sevikas receive comprehensive training on various aspects including child development, health and nutrition, early childhood education, and community mobilization. They also undergo regular refresher courses to update their knowledge and skills. Additionally, they receive support from supervisors, resource persons, and government programs to effectively carry out their duties and responsibilities

Can I volunteer at an Anganwadi center??

You can express your interest in volunteering at an Anganwadi center by sending your details via email. We will review your information and add you to our volunteer group as per the requirements
